vRTS

The project

HackDuke 2013. Augmented reality Android app that allows two phones to view the same virtual object with their cameras from different angles. Proof-of-concept for a virtual reality real-time strategy (vRTS) game, among other applications. We placed top 10 out of 102 groups and won second place in Epic Games' Best Game prize.

My role

My partners were much stronger coders than I, so I scoured the OpenCV libraries to determine which algorithms (and in what order) to use for the image processing. When we were chosen to demo the project, I led the presentation.
