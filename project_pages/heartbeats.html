Heart Beats

The project

Select music to play for pediatric patients based on their heart rate. This was a class project for Medical Instrumentation.

My role

My group included three third-year biomedical engineering students. I programmed the LabVIEW system that filtered the cardiac signal, determined its frequency, and selected music based on the number of beats per minute. I also programmed the LabVIEW user interface and helped design the electrocardiogram (ECG) circuit.

Result

We created a prototype system that takes ECG readings and chooses one of three songs to play.
