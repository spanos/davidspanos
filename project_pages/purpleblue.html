"Purpl3lue": Group management and messaging app

The project

Organizing HackNC had left me in want of a simple but effective group management app. At Bitcamp (the University of Maryland's hackathon), two friends and I took it upon ourselves to make one. We spent the 36-hour period working on the node.js and MongoDB backend, messaging functionality using socket.io, and Heroku for deployment. None of us had used any of these technologies before. After Bitcamp we continued the project for several weeks. I would like to revisit it eventually, but Purpl3lue (our nonsensical codename for the app) has been shelved for now.
