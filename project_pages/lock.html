Lock Project

The project

Create a "lock box" with two switches and two buttons that, when activated in the proper order, "unlock" the box in three stages. Each stage has its own requirements and limitations for logic devices. This was an individual class project for Electronics II, and certainly my most challenging academic assignment to date.

Result

I designed each stage's layout and timing diagram on paper and simulated them on Multisim (pictured above). Once the entire circuit worked on a breadboard I transfered it to circuit boards. The final product consisted of a complex arrangement of analog and digital circuitry housed in a 2.5" x 3" x 5" box.
