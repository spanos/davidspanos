Syringe Pump

The project

Given two months and a $100 budget, create a syringe pump capable of automatically delivering 1 to 10 mL of fluid at a steady rate. This was a class project for Biomedical Engineering Design and Manufacturing II.

My role

My group included four junior biomedical engineering students; it was one of the smoothest group operations I have ever experienced. I programmed the motor controller (PIC16F84A) in C and helped with other miscellaneous tasks.

Result

We finished well ahead of schedule, and our professors have used our device as an example for subsequent classes.
