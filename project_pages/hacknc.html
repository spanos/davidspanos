HackNC

The goal

Foster a stronger community among UNC computer science students and establish UNC in the national hackathon scene.

My role

HackNC was my crowning achievement in the 2013-2014 school year. As Lead Organizer, I worked with a group of 10 student organizers and the UNC Computer Science Department while managing sponsorship, budgeting, branding, outreach to other universities, and transportation.

Result

UNC's previous hackathon consisted of 30 students and one sponsor. HackNC brought together 200 students and 20 mentors from across the Southeast to become our largest hackathon ever. The event has been passed down to the next generation of student leaders, who are currently planning HackNC Fall 2014.
